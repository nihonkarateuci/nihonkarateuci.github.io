Sensei Bruce Nguyen

Sensei Bruce Nguyen

Sensei Bruce Nguyen is head instructor at the Nihon Karate Dojo and co-owner of NKD Martial Arts. Sensei Nguyen is head instructor of the University of California, Irvine karate class and team, and is an adviser for the UCI karate club. He has been teaching and training under Ryobu-Kai for the past 20 years and holds a 4th degree black belt. In addition, he has comprehensive experience with instructing military and law enforcement agents. Sensei Nguyen has competed both nationally and internationally. He holds a position in the Masters' Hall of Fame as Instructor of the Year for 2006 due to his achievements within the martial arts community.
